Low Carbon

Creating websites that are good for our planet is really important. We can do this by using simpler pictures (dithered images), sticking to basic fonts and not using fancy logos, and not having unnecessary ads, cookies or tracking. This means websites can load faster, use less energy, and be more user-friendly. By making content simple and easy to interact with, we also help in reducing the amount of energy used when people browse the web. Having eco-friendly web design is very important because it helps in saving energy, giving users a better experience, and keeping the planet healthy. It’s not just a passing trend; it’s a necessary change in the way we think about and create digital products. In today’s world, where almost everything is online, it’s crucial for web designers to think about the planet and make their websites more sustainable.
